My Journey

2021

The Beginning

My journey as a VTuber started with a simple idea - to create a digital persona that could connect with people around the world. I designed my first model and started streaming on YouTube.

At first, it was just me and a few viewers, but we quickly formed a small community of like-minded individuals.

2022

First Original Song

After months of preparation, I released my first original song "Starlight Dreams". The response was overwhelming, with over 100k views in the first week.

This marked a turning point in my career as I began focusing more on music creation alongside streaming.

2023

Community Growth

My community grew to over 50,000 subscribers across platforms. I launched my first merchandise line which sold out within 24 hours.

I also collaborated with other popular VTubers, expanding my reach and creating unique content for my fans.

2024

International Recognition

This year marked my first international tour with virtual concerts in Japan, Thailand, and South Korea. I released my first EP "Digital Heart" which charted in multiple countries.

I'm currently working on a new model with advanced tracking for more expressive performances.
